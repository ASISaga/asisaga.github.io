Startup Metrics Dashboard

Retention Rate

Percentage of users staying with your product over time.

Net Dollar Retention (NDR)

Revenue retained and expanded from existing customers.

Gross Margin

Profitability of core business operations.

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

Cost of acquiring a new customer.

Lifetime Value (LTV)

Total revenue generated by a customer over time.

Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)

Predictable revenue from subscription-based businesses.

Churn Rate

Percentage of users who stop using your product.

Growth Rate

Speed at which your revenue or user base is growing.
